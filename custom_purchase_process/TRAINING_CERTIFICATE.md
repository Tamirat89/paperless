## SYSTEM COMPONENTS COVERED

### 1. PURCHASE REQUEST MANAGEMENT
- Creating and managing purchase requests
- Department-based approval workflows
- Request tracking and status monitoring
- Company isolation and access control

### 2. MATERIAL REQUEST SYSTEM  
- Material requisition processes
- Approval hierarchies
- Stock integration
- Request fulfillment tracking

### 3. SUPPLIER ANALYSIS WORKFLOW
- Supplier evaluation processes
- Committee approval mechanisms
- Quality inspection requirements
- Multi-stage approval workflow (Request → In Progress → Committee Approved → GM Approved)

### 4. QUALITY INSPECTION MANAGEMENT
- Quality assurance integration
- Inspection scheduling and tracking
- QA requirement validation
- Quality inspection sequences (QI-00001 format)

### 5. STORE ISSUE VOUCHER (SIV)
- SIV creation and management
- Date tracking and documentation
- Store inventory management

### 6. BANK RECONCILIATION
- Activity management
- Approval and rejection workflows
- Financial reconciliation processes

## USER ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

| **Role** | **Access Level** | **Key Responsibilities** |
|----------|------------------|--------------------------|
| **Requester** | Own requests only | Create requests, track status, provide additional information |
| **Department Approver** | Department requests | Review and approve/reject department requests |
| **Store Manager** | All department requests | Manage store operations, oversee all requests |
| **System Administrator** | Full system access | System configuration, user management, troubleshooting |
| **Quality Assurance** | QA-related records | Quality inspections, validation, compliance checking |
| **Committee Members** | Committee-level approvals | Strategic approval decisions, budget oversight |
| **General Manager** | Final approvals | Executive approval authority, final decision making |
